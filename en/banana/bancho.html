What is Top Banana?

「I keep this bunch in line」

Top Banana is the BGMT type in the 16 Banana Type Test. A member of The Thinkers (Logic and curiosity — the brains that ripen the world), with a rarity of 4.1% (banana census). This page covers their personality, classic moments, compatibility, and love & work styles.

Rules matter; loyalty matters more. Fights injustice head-on and would take a bruise for the crew without saying a word about it.

Classic Top Banana moments

  • Physically incapable of ignoring someone cutting in line

Owner's Manual

  1. Play it straight with them and you gain the strongest ally there is

Love & Work

Love style

Blunt but utterly single-hearted. Love shows up in actions rather than words, so watch what they do, not what they say.

Work style

Shines wherever discipline matters. Natural fit for quality control, training juniors, and site supervision.
